National Pledge

By Ajiesh.V

India is my Country.All Indians are my
brothers and sisters.I Love my Country and
I'm proud of it's rich and varied heritage.
I shall give my parents,teachers and all my elders respect
and treat everyone with courtesy.To my country and
my people, I pledge my devotion. In their wellbeing and prosperity alone, lies
my happiness.
Jay Hind!
